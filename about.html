About FUSION 2024

FUSION 2024 is a forward-thinking scientific event focused on Astronomy, Astrophysics, AI, Biotechnology, Environmental Science, Nanoscience, Quantum Computing, and Renewable Energy. Celebrating National Science Day, the seminar bridges theory and practice, involving students and experts.

The proceedings, published in both print and digital formats, promise valuable insights. FUSION 2024's national significance lies in its role as a catalyst for innovation, aligning with India's goals in research, technology, and sustainable development.

Under the theme "Frontiers Unveiled," FUSION 2024 aims to inspire collaboration, knowledge exchange, and the growth of science in India and beyond.

 

About the Institute

Prof. Rajendra Singh (Rajju Bhaiya) Institute of Physical Sciences for Study and Research within the campus of Veer Bahadur Singh Purvanchal University, Jaunpur is an Institution of national reputation and established by the eﬀorts of the Government of Uttar Pradesh, in the year 2018. The Institute is a tribute to visionary Prof. Rajendra Singh popularly known as 'Rajju Bhaiya', who was a Distinguished Professor and Head of the Physics Department, University of Allahabad, Prayagraj. Rajju Bhaiya was not only an educationist but also a thinker, a social reformer and a skilled organizer.

The establishment of the Institute accomplishes the mission of the University to oﬀer best environment for education, students and society for universal developments in all respects. The Institute has four departments of Physical Sciences viz: Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics and Earth & Planetary Sciences which oﬀers the undergraduate, postgraduate and doctoral programme. Institute has two research centers i.e., Nanoscience and Technology Research Centre and Renewable Energy Research Centre which oﬀer M. Tech. (Materials Science & Technology) programme. The departments and research centres have well-equipped laboratories with instruments like SEM-EDS, XRD, FTIR, UV Vis, APS-100, TPS-500S with other infrastructural facilities for undertaking advanced level research.
